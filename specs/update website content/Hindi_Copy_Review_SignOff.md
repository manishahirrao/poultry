## Review Criteria Compliance

### Language Register & Tone
- [ ] No overly formal "Shuddh Hindi" that farmers won't recognize
- [ ] Colloquial register appropriate for Gorakhpur farming community
- [ ] Direct and simple sentences (suitable for mobile reading)
- [ ] Appropriate use of Hinglish (Hindi-English mix) where natural

### Number Format
- [ ] All monetary values use Indian numbering format (lakhs, crores)
- [ ] Consistent use of Hindi number words (हजार, लाख) where appropriate
- [ ] No Western format numbers (100,000) in farmer-facing copy

### Agricultural Terminology
- [ ] Feed terms use what farmers actually say (e.g., "चारा" not "पशु आहार")
- [ ] Bird terms use local terminology
- [ ] Disease terms use common names farmers recognize
- [ ] Operations terms use everyday farming language

### Abbreviations & Technical Terms
- [ ] FCR explained or used with context
- [ ] HPAI explained or paired with "बर्ड फ्लू"
- [ ] Other technical terms (MAPE, P10/P50/P90) have appropriate explanations
- [ ] English words in Hindi are natural and necessary (WhatsApp, app, etc.)

### Sentence Structure & Readability
- [ ] Sentence length appropriate for mobile reading (max 15-20 words)
- [ ] Active voice used predominantly
- [ ] Clear, unambiguous language
- [ ] No complex clauses or nested structures
