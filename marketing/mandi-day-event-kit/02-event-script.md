## Objection Handling (Throughout)
**Goal**: Address common concerns

### "बहुत महंगा है" (Too expensive)
**Hindi**: "₹67/दिन — 100 पक्षियों के लिए चारा से भी कम। 
25,000 पक्षियों पर, ₹1/kg timing loss बचाना = ₹25,000 per batch 
(1 साल की subscription एक batch में!)"

**English**: "₹67/day — less than feed cost for 100 birds. 
On 25,000 birds, saving ₹1/kg timing loss = ₹25,000 per batch 
(1 year subscription in ONE batch!)"

### "WhatsApp groups free हैं" (WhatsApp groups are free)
**Hindi**: "Groups कल का भाव बताते हैं — हम अगले हफ्ते का। 
वह 5 दिन का difference जहां आप ₹50,000+ कमाते या गंवाते हैं।"

**English**: "Groups tell yesterday's price — we tell next week's. 
That 5-day difference is where you make or lose ₹50,000+."

### "AI सटीक नहीं होगा" (AI won't be accurate)
**Hindi**: "95.2% accuracy on 6-month Gorakhpur data। 
आज हमने दिखाया — सिर्फ ₹1 difference! 
Public accuracy dashboard हर दिन का performance दिखाता है।"

**English**: "95.2% accuracy on 6-month Gorakhpur data. 
We showed it today — only ₹1 difference! 
Public accuracy dashboard shows every day's performance."

## Tips for Delivery
- Speak clearly and slowly
- Use local examples and names
- Maintain eye contact
- Be patient with questions
- Have calculator ready for ROI calculations
- Keep energy high throughout
- Use hand gestures for emphasis
- Smile and be approachable
