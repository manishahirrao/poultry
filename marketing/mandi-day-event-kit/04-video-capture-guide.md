# Video Capture Guide
**How to film farmer testimonials at mandi events**

## Equipment Needed
- **Smartphone** (iPhone 12+ or Android equivalent)
- **External microphone** (lapel mic or shotgun mic - optional but recommended)
- **Tripod** or stable surface
- **Portable light** (for indoor/low-light situations)
- **Power bank** (fully charged)
- **Clean microfiber cloth** (for lens cleaning)

## Pre-Interview Preparation
### Before Recording
- [ ] Test audio recording in mandi environment
- [ ] Find quiet location away from traffic/noise
- [ ] Clean phone camera lens
- [ ] Ensure sufficient storage space (1GB+)
- [ ] Set phone to airplane mode to avoid interruptions
- [ ] Check battery level (80%+)
- [ ] Prepare consent form (verbal or written)

### Consent Process
**Hindi Script**:
```
क्या हम आपकी video ले सकते हैं? 
हम इसे WhatsApp पर share करेंगे ताकि दूसरे किसान भी जानें।
आपका नाम और location भी बताएंगे।
ठीक है?
```

**English Script**:
```
Can we record your video?
We'll share it on WhatsApp so other farmers can know.
We'll also share your name and location.
Is that okay?
```

## Framing and Composition
### Camera Settings
- **Resolution**: 1080p at 30fps (minimum)
- **Orientation**: Horizontal (landscape) for social media
- **Grid**: Enable grid lines for better composition
- **HDR**: Off (can cause audio sync issues)
- **Stabilization**: On if available

### Framing Guidelines
- **Subject distance**: 3-5 feet from camera
- **Eye level**: Camera at subject's eye level
- **Headroom**: Leave 10% space above head
- **Background**: Simple, relevant (mandi, birds, farm)
- **Lighting**: Natural light facing subject (avoid backlight)

### Shot Types
1. **Talking head** (primary): Waist-up shot, subject speaking
2. **B-roll**: Hands working, farm operations, mandi activity
3. **Establishing shot**: Wide shot of mandi/farm location
4. **Detail shots**: Price board, phone showing app, birds

## Interview Questions
### Opening Questions (Warm-up)
1. "आपका नाम क्या है? कहां से हैं?" (What's your name? Where are you from?)
2. "कितने पक्षी हैं आपके पास?" (How many birds do you have?)
3. "कितने साल से कर रहे हैं poultry farming?" (How long have you been doing poultry farming?)

### Problem Questions
4. "पहले बेचते समय क्या problem होती थी?" (What problem did you face when selling before?)
5. "कभी timing से बेचने में loss हुआ है?" (Have you ever had losses from selling at the wrong time?)
6. "WhatsApp groups से क्या मिलता था?" (What did you get from WhatsApp groups?)

### Solution Questions
7. "PoultryPulse AI कैसे use करते हैं?" (How do you use PoultryPulse AI?)
8. "WhatsApp पर signal कैसा आता है?" (How does the signal come on WhatsApp?)
9. "7 दिन का भाव जानकर कैसा लगता है?" (How does it feel knowing the 7-day price?)

### Results Questions
10. "कितना बचाया है PoultryPulse से?" (How much have you saved with PoultryPulse?)
11. "कोई specific example दे सकते हैं?" (Can you give a specific example?)
12. "Disease alert का क्या फायदा हुआ?" (What benefit did you get from disease alerts?)

### Closing Questions
13. "दूसरे किसानों को क्या message देना चाहेंगे?" (What message would you give to other farmers?)
14. "PoultryPulse AI recommend करेंगे?" (Would you recommend PoultryPulse AI?)

## Recording Tips
### During Recording
- [ ] Hold phone steady or use tripod
- [ ] Maintain eye contact with subject (not screen)
- [ ] Nod and show engagement (don't interrupt)
- [ ] Record each answer in full (even if long)
- [ ] Ask follow-up questions naturally
- [ ] Keep background noise minimal
- [ ] Monitor audio levels (watch waveform)

### Audio Best Practices
- [ ] Use lapel mic if available (clip 6-8 inches from mouth)
- [ ] If no mic, hold phone 6-12 inches from subject
- [ ] Avoid windy locations (use windscreen if needed)
- [ ] Record test audio first
- [ ] Speak clearly and at moderate pace
- [ ] Allow natural pauses (don't rush)

### Lighting Tips
- [ ] Face subject toward natural light (window, open sky)
- [ ] Avoid direct sunlight (causes harsh shadows)
- [ ] Use shade or overcast conditions
- [ ] If indoors, use portable light facing subject
- [ ] Avoid mixed lighting (sunlight + artificial)

## Post-Recording Processing
### Immediate Actions
- [ ] Label videos with farmer name and location
- [ ] Back up to cloud storage immediately
- [ ] Note timestamp of best quotes
- [ ] Document consent status

### Editing Guidelines
**Keep it short**: 30-60 seconds maximum
**Best structure**:
1. Hook (problem): "I lost ₹50K from bad timing..."
2. Solution: "Then PoultryPulse told me 7 days ahead..."
3. Result: "I saved ₹1.2L in 6 months..."
4. Call to action: "Every farmer should try this"

**Editing tips**:
- Add Hindi subtitles (essential for WhatsApp sharing)
- Include PoultryPulse logo at end
- Add location text overlay (e.g., "Gorakhpur, UP")
- Use simple transitions (fade, no fancy effects)
- Keep original audio (no background music)
- Export in 720p for WhatsApp compatibility

### WhatsApp Sharing Format
- **Resolution**: 720p (1280x720)
- **Format**: MP4
- **Duration**: Under 60 seconds
- **File size**: Under 50MB (WhatsApp limit)
- **Aspect ratio**: 16:9 (horizontal)
- **Bitrate**: 2-3 Mbps

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
❌ **Don't**: Record with phone vertical (portrait mode)
❌ **Don't**: Put subject with bright light behind them
❌ **Don't**: Record in noisy locations (near traffic, machinery)
❌ **Don't**: Interrupt or rush the subject
❌ **Don't**: Use fancy transitions or effects
❌ **Don't** Add background music (distracts from message)
❌ **Don't**: Record without consent
❌ **Don't**: Share videos longer than 60 seconds
