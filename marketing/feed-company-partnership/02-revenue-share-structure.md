# Revenue Share Structure
**Commission tiers and payment schedule for feed company partners**

## Commission Tiers

### Year 1: 20% Commission
- **Rate**: 20% of first-year subscription revenue
- **Eligibility**: All referred customers who convert to paid subscription
- **Calculation**: Monthly subscription × 12 months × 20%
- **Example**: ₹2,000/month × 12 = ₹24,000 × 20% = ₹4,800 commission per customer

### Year 2: 10% Commission
- **Rate**: 10% of second-year subscription revenue
- **Eligibility**: Customers who renew subscription
- **Calculation**: Monthly subscription × 12 months × 10%
- **Example**: ₹2,000/month × 12 = ₹24,000 × 10% = ₹2,400 commission per customer

### Year 3: 5% Commission
- **Rate**: 5% of third-year subscription revenue
- **Eligibility**: Customers who renew subscription
- **Calculation**: Monthly subscription × 12 months × 5%
- **Example**: ₹2,000/month × 12 = ₹24,000 × 5% = ₹1,200 commission per customer

## Performance Bonuses

### Tier 1: Bronze (20-39 leads/month)
- **Base Commission**: As per year structure
- **Bonus**: +2% commission on all conversions that month
- **Threshold**: Minimum 20 qualified leads

### Tier 2: Silver (40-59 leads/month)
- **Base Commission**: As per year structure
- **Bonus**: +5% commission on all conversions that month
- **Threshold**: Minimum 40 qualified leads

### Tier 3: Gold (60+ leads/month)
- **Base Commission**: As per year structure
- **Bonus**: +8% commission on all conversions that month
- **Threshold**: Minimum 60 qualified leads

### Exclusivity Bonus
- **Additional**: +3% commission for partners with district exclusivity
- **Condition**: Must meet minimum lead requirements for 3 consecutive months

## Payment Schedule

### Monthly Calculation
- **Cutoff Date**: Last day of each month
- **Calculation Window**: 1st to last day of month
- **Report Generation**: By 5th of following month
- **Partner Review**: 5th-10th of following month
- **Payment**: By 15th of following month

### Payment Methods
- **Bank Transfer**: Preferred for large amounts (₹10,000+)
- **UPI**: For amounts under ₹10,000
- **Cheque**: Available upon request

### Minimum Payout Threshold
- **Minimum**: ₹5,000 per payment
- **Rollover**: Amounts below ₹5,000 roll over to next month
- **Maximum Rollover**: 3 months (after which payment is forced)

## Commission Calculation Examples

### Example 1: New Partner (Bronze Tier)
**Month 1 Performance**:
- Qualified leads: 25
- Trial signups: 20 (80% conversion)
- Paid conversions: 4 (20% trial-to-paid)
- Average subscription: ₹2,000/month

**Commission Calculation**:
- Revenue: 4 × ₹24,000 = ₹96,000
- Base commission (20%): ₹19,200
- Bronze bonus (+2%): ₹1,920
- **Total**: ₹21,120

### Example 2: High-Performing Partner (Gold Tier)
**Month 1 Performance**:
- Qualified leads: 65
- Trial signups: 55 (85% conversion)
- Paid conversions: 12 (22% trial-to-paid)
- Average subscription: ₹2,500/month (mix of PulseFarm and PulsePro)

**Commission Calculation**:
- Revenue: 12 × ₹30,000 = ₹3,60,000
- Base commission (20%): ₹72,000
- Gold bonus (+8%): ₹28,800
- Exclusivity bonus (+3%): ₹10,800
- **Total**: ₹1,11,600

### Example 3: Year 2 Earnings
**Scenario**: Partner referred 50 customers in Year 1, 40 renew in Year 2

**Year 1 Earnings**:
- 50 × ₹24,000 = ₹12,00,000 revenue
- 20% commission = ₹2,40,000

**Year 2 Earnings**:
- 40 × ₹24,000 = ₹9,60,000 revenue
- 10% commission = ₹96,000

**Total 2-Year Earnings**: ₹3,36,000

## Tracking and Reporting

### Referral Tracking
- **Method**: Unique QR codes and referral links
- **Attribution Window**: 90 days from first click
- **Multi-touch**: Last touch attribution (most recent partner gets credit)
- **Self-referrals**: Not eligible for commission

### Monthly Commission Report
Partner A will provide monthly report including:
- Total qualified leads received
- Trial signups
- Paid conversions
- Revenue generated
- Commission calculation
- Bonus eligibility
- Payment amount

### Audit Rights
- Partner B may audit referral data with 7-day written notice
- Audit limited to referred customers only
- Audit costs borne by Partner B unless errors found
- Discrepancies >5% trigger full audit

## Commission Disputes

### Resolution Process
1. Partner B raises dispute within 10 days of report
2. Partner A provides supporting documentation within 5 days
3. Good faith negotiation for 10 days
4. If unresolved, third-party audit (split cost)
5. Final decision binding on both parties

### Common Dispute Scenarios
- **Duplicate leads**: First timestamp wins
- **Customer already known**: Not eligible for commission
- **Self-referral**: Not eligible, commission forfeited
- **Late attribution**: Outside 90-day window not eligible

## Tax and Compliance

### Tax Deduction
- Partner A deducts TDS as per Indian tax laws
- Form 16A provided to Partner B annually
- Partner B responsible for filing income tax

### GST Considerations
- Commission payments may attract GST
- Partner A provides GST invoice
- Partner B can claim input tax credit

### Compliance
- Both Parties comply with Indian tax laws
- Proper documentation maintained for 7 years
- Annual tax summary provided by Partner A

## Special Provisions

### Early Termination
- Outstanding commissions paid within 30 days
- No future commissions on terminated customers
- Partner B may retain marketing materials for 30 days

### Change in Pricing
- Commission calculated on actual revenue received
- Price changes apply to new customers only
- Existing customers locked in at original rate for commission purposes

### Bundle Promotions
- Special promotional pricing handled case-by-case
- Commission percentage may be adjusted for deep discounts
- Must be agreed in writing before promotion launch
