# Partnership Agreement Template
**PoultryPulse AI × [Feed Company Name]**

## Partnership Agreement

**This Partnership Agreement** ("Agreement") is entered into on [Date], by and between:

**PoultryPulse AI** ("Partner A"), a company incorporated under the laws of India, with its registered office at [Address], represented by [Name], [Title]

AND

**[Feed Company Name]** ("Partner B"), a company incorporated under the laws of India, with its registered office at [Address], represented by [Name], [Title]

(Collectively referred to as the "Parties")

## 1. Purpose
The Parties agree to collaborate on a co-marketing partnership to promote PoultryPulse AI's price intelligence services to Partner B's customer base of commercial poultry farmers.

## 2. Term
This Agreement shall commence on [Start Date] and continue for a period of 12 months ("Initial Term"), unless terminated earlier as provided herein. Thereafter, the Agreement shall automatically renew for successive 12-month periods unless either Party provides 30 days written notice of non-renewal.

## 3. Territory
Partner B shall have exclusive rights to promote PoultryPulse AI services in the [District Name] district for the first 6 months of this Agreement, provided Partner B meets minimum performance metrics (Section 6).

## 4. Partner B Obligations

### 4.1 Bundle Offer
Partner B agrees to offer a 14-day free trial of PoultryPulse AI services to customers who purchase feed orders of ₹50,000 or more.

### 4.2 Marketing Materials
Partner B agrees to:
- Display PoultryPulse AI marketing materials at their retail locations
- Include PoultryPulse AI information in customer communications
- Share co-branded content on their WhatsApp groups and social media
- Train sales staff on PoultryPulse AI value proposition

### 4.3 Lead Generation
Partner B agrees to:
- Collect customer contact information for PoultryPulse AI trial signups
- Forward qualified leads to Partner A within 24 hours
- Provide basic customer context (farm size, location, current challenges)

### 4.4 Brand Guidelines
Partner B agrees to use PoultryPulse AI brand materials only as provided and not modify them without prior written consent.

## 5. Partner A Obligations

### 5.1 Trial Setup
Partner A agrees to:
- Provide 14-day free trial access to all referred customers
- Set up accounts within 24 hours of lead receipt
- Provide onboarding support in Hindi
- Ensure WhatsApp delivery of price signals

### 5.2 Marketing Support
Partner A agrees to:
- Provide co-branded marketing materials
- Create custom QR codes for Partner B tracking
- Offer training sessions for Partner B sales team
- Provide sales scripts and objection handling guides

### 5.3 Technical Support
Partner A agrees to:
- Provide dedicated support channel for Partner B
- Respond to partner inquiries within 4 hours
- Maintain 95%+ service availability
- Provide accuracy dashboard access

## 6. Performance Metrics

### 6.1 Minimum Requirements
To maintain exclusivity, Partner B must achieve:
- **Month 1-3**: Minimum 20 qualified leads per month
- **Month 4-6**: Minimum 30 qualified leads per month
- **Month 7-12**: Minimum 40 qualified leads per month

### 6.2 Conversion Targets
Target conversion rates:
- Trial signup to paid subscription: 15-20%
- Partner B leads to trial signup: 60-80%

### 6.3 Review Periods
Performance will be reviewed quarterly. If Partner B fails to meet minimum requirements for two consecutive quarters, Partner A may terminate exclusivity.

## 7. Revenue Share

### 7.1 Commission Structure
Partner B shall receive:
- **20%** of first-year subscription revenue from referred customers
- **10%** of second-year subscription revenue from referred customers
- **5%** of third-year subscription revenue from referred customers

### 7.2 Payment Terms
- Commissions calculated monthly
- Payments made within 15 days of month-end
- Minimum payout threshold: ₹5,000
- Payment via bank transfer or UPI

### 7.3 Tracking
- All referrals tracked via unique QR codes/links
- Partner A provides monthly commission report
- Partner B has right to audit referral data with 7-day notice

## 8. Intellectual Property

### 8.1 Ownership
Each Party retains ownership of their pre-existing intellectual property.

### 8.2 Joint Materials
Co-branded materials created during this partnership shall be jointly owned, with both Parties having right to use for partnership purposes.

### 8.3 License
Each Party grants the other a non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use their trademarks and logos solely for purposes of this Agreement.

## 9. Confidentiality

Both Parties agree to:
- Keep all confidential information confidential
- Not disclose customer data without consent
- Use customer data only for partnership purposes
- Comply with DPDP Act 2023 requirements

## 10. Termination

### 10.1 Termination for Cause
Either Party may terminate this Agreement upon 30 days written notice if the other Party materially breaches any provision of this Agreement.

### 10.2 Immediate Termination
Either Party may terminate immediately if the other Party:
- Engages in fraudulent activity
- Violates data privacy laws
- Damages the other Party's reputation

### 10.3 Effect of Termination
Upon termination:
- Outstanding commissions will be paid within 30 days
- Customer accounts will continue as per customer agreements
- Marketing materials must be removed within 7 days
- Confidential information must be returned or destroyed

## 11. Liability and Indemnification

### 11.1 Limitation of Liability
Neither Party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages. Total liability shall not exceed total commissions paid in the preceding 12 months.

### 11.2 Indemnification
Each Party agrees to indemnify the other for claims arising from their own negligence or willful misconduct.

## 12. Governing Law and Dispute Resolution

### 12.1 Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India.

### 12.2 Dispute Resolution
Disputes shall be resolved through good faith negotiations. If unresolved, disputes shall be referred to arbitration in [City], India.

## 13. Miscellaneous

### 13.1 Entire Agreement
This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the Parties and supersedes all prior discussions.

### 13.2 Amendments
Any amendments must be in writing and signed by both Parties.

### 13.3 Assignment
Neither Party may assign this Agreement without prior written consent.

### 13.4 Severability
If any provision is found invalid, the remaining provisions shall remain in effect.

### 13.5 Notices
All notices shall be in writing and delivered to:
- **Partner A**: [Email] / [Address]
- **Partner B**: [Email] / [Address]
