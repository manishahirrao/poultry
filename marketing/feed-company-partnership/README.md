# Feed Company Partnership Kit
**Marketing Initiative #5: Partner with Feed Companies for Co-Marketing**

## Overview
Partner with feed companies serving Gorakhpur belt to bundle PoultryPulse AI trial with feed purchases. Feed companies have direct access to target customers and aligned incentives (healthy farms = more feed sales).

## Partnership Model
- **Bundle Offer**: 14-day PoultryPulse AI trial with every ₹50K+ feed order
- **Revenue Share**: 20% of first-year subscription revenue from referred customers
- **Co-Branding**: Feed company logo on marketing materials
- **Exclusive Territory**: First partner in each district gets exclusivity for 6 months

## Materials Included
1. **Partnership Agreement Template** - Legal agreement with terms and conditions
2. **Co-Branded Collateral** - One-pagers, banners, WhatsApp messages
3. **Revenue Share Structure** - Commission tiers and payment schedule
4. **Implementation Guide** - How to integrate into feed company sales process
5. **Sales Script** - Talking points for feed company sales team

## Target Partners
- Major feed companies in Gorakhpur belt
- Regional feed distributors
- Integrator feed suppliers
- Veterinary supply companies

## Expected Outcomes
- 50-100 qualified leads per partner per month
- 15-20% conversion rate (warm leads)
- ₹2-4 lakh additional ARR per partner
- Expanded brand visibility through feed company network

## Success Metrics
- Number of partnerships signed
- Leads generated per partner
- Conversion rate from partner leads
- Revenue share paid to partners
- Partner satisfaction score
