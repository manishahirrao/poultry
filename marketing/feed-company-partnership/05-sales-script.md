## Tips for Delivery

### Do's
- ✅ Speak clearly and at moderate pace
- ✅ Maintain eye contact
- ✅ Use local examples and names
- ✅ Be patient with questions
- ✅ Show confidence in the product
- ✅ Keep it conversational, not scripted
- ✅ Use hand gestures for emphasis
- ✅ Smile and be approachable

### Don'ts
- ❌ Don't be pushy or aggressive
- ❌ Don't interrupt the customer
- ❌ Don't use technical jargon
- ❌ Don't make unrealistic promises
- ❌ Don't rush the customer
- ❌ Don't sound robotic
- ❌ Don't ignore objections
- ❌ Don't give up too easily

### Body Language
- Stand at appropriate distance (2-3 feet)
- Face the customer directly
- Nod when customer speaks
- Use open gestures (palms visible)
- Maintain friendly facial expression
- Point to QR code stand when mentioning it

### Voice Tone
- Warm and friendly
- Confident but not arrogant
- Enthusiastic about the value
- Patient when explaining
- Clear and articulate
- Natural and conversational
