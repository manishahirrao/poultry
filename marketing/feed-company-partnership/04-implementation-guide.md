## Integration Points

### Point 1: At Checkout
**Trigger**: Customer places ₹50K+ feed order

**Process**:
1. Staff identifies eligible customer
2. Staff mentions free trial offer
3. Staff helps customer activate (scan QR or provide number)
4. Staff confirms activation successful
5. Customer receives welcome WhatsApp

**Time**: 2-3 minutes

**Staff Training**: Essential - all counter staff must be trained

### Point 2: Post-Purchase Follow-up
**Trigger**: Feed order delivered (24-48 hours later)

**Process**:
1. Automated WhatsApp message sent
2. Reminder to activate trial if not done
3. Link to activation page
4. Offer to help if needed

**Automation**: Fully automated via PoultryPulse AI system

### Point 3: Regular Communication
**Trigger**: Weekly customer engagement

**Process**:
1. Weekly WhatsApp tips on using PoultryPulse
2. Success stories from other farmers
3. Reminder of trial expiration
4. Upgrade offer before trial ends

**Automation**: Semi-automated with personalization

## Troubleshooting Guide

### Issue: Low Activation Rate
**Possible Causes**:
- Staff not mentioning offer
- Customers not interested
- Technical issues with activation

**Solutions**:
- Retrain staff
- Improve messaging
- Simplify activation process
- Add incentive for staff

### Issue: Low Conversion Rate
**Possible Causes**:
- Trial not valuable enough
- Onboarding poor
- Price too high

**Solutions**:
- Improve onboarding experience
- Add more value during trial
- Offer discount at trial end
- Provide success stories

### Issue: Technical Issues
**Possible Causes**:
- QR code not working
- WhatsApp not delivering
- System downtime

**Solutions**:
- Have backup activation method
- Manual activation option
- Immediate support contact
- System monitoring

## Success Factors

### Critical Success Factors
1. **Staff Buy-in**: Staff must believe in the product
2. **Customer Trust**: Feed company endorsement is powerful
3. **Smooth Activation**: Frictionless activation process
4. **Clear Value**: Customers must see immediate value
5. **Ongoing Support**: Both partners must be responsive

### Common Pitfalls to Avoid
1. **Pushy Sales**: Don't force it, make it a natural offer
2. **Complex Process**: Keep activation simple
3. **Poor Training**: Under-trained staff = poor results
4. **No Follow-up**: Trial end needs attention
5. **Ignoring Feedback**: Listen and adapt
