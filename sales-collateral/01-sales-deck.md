## Slide 4: Our Solution — AI-Powered Price Intelligence

**Headline**: 7-Day Price Predictions with 95%+ Accuracy  

**How It Works**:
1. **Data Collection**: 47 public sources (AGMARKNET, NECC, IMD weather, feed prices) — updated daily at 4:30 AM
2. **AI Prediction**: LightGBM + Temporal Fusion Transformer ensemble — predicts 7-day price movements
3. **Delivery**: WhatsApp + App at 6:30 AM every morning

**Key Differentiators**:
- **7-day forward window** (industry standard: 1–2 days)
- **95.2% directional accuracy** on 6-month Gorakhpur holdout data
- **WhatsApp-first** — no new app to learn
- **Hindi-first** — designed for farmers, not techies

**Speaker Notes**:
- We use only public, zero-cost data — no proprietary feeds
- Same class of models used by commodity trading desks
- Farmers don't need to learn anything new — they already use WhatsApp
