## Design Context

### Users
Indian poultry integration companies (S2 segment) managing 50K–500K birds across multiple farms. Hindi and English speaking, operations managers and owners who use both desktop dashboards and mobile devices. They manage ₹2–10 crore annual business, oversee multiple farm locations, coordinate with farm managers, and need portfolio-level intelligence to optimize operations across their entire flock. They make decisions affecting dozens of farms and thousands of birds daily.

### Brand Personality
Professional, data-driven, enterprise-grade agricultural intelligence. Three words: **Enterprise precision**, **Portfolio intelligence**, **Operational excellence**. The interface should evoke confidence, control, and comprehensive oversight - integration companies manage complex operations and need trustworthy data at scale.

### Aesthetic Direction
Data-dense B2B dashboard with agricultural earth tones, optimized for quick scanning across multiple metrics. Brand colors: warm orange-red (brandOrange700 #D4551A) as primary brand color derived from the rooster logo, forest green (brandGreen700 #1A6B3C) as secondary for agricultural context, saffron orange (#E8621A) for urgency/alerts, amber (#F5A623) for warnings, with additional status colors for FCR/mortality bands. Typography: Noto Sans Devanagari for Hindi text, Space Grotesk/Plus Jakarta Sans for English. Clean, professional dashboard aesthetic - serious business tool for serious operations. No gradients on text, no glassmorphism, no AI slop aesthetics.

### Design Principles
1. **Portfolio-First** - Show the big picture first. Integration companies need to see across all farms before diving into details.
2. **Data Density with Clarity** - More information per screen than farmer UI, but still scannable. Use visual hierarchy, not clutter.
3. **Bilingual Interface** - Hindi and English both primary. Users switch between languages based on context and preference.
4. **Cross-Device Continuity** - Start analysis on desktop, continue monitoring on mobile. Seamless experience.
5. **Actionable Intelligence** - Every metric should suggest an action or insight. Not just data, but direction.
6. **Real-Time Awareness** - Portfolio changes, alerts, and issues should be visible without page refresh.

### Technical Constraints
- WCAG 2.1 AA minimum (touch targets ≥48×48dp, contrast ≥4.5:1)
- Responsive: desktop-first dashboard that adapts to tablet/mobile
- Design tokens in packages/ui/src/tokens.ts - no hardcoded values
- Noto Sans Devanagari mandatory for Hindi text
- Real-time updates via Supabase Realtime for critical alerts
- Performance budgets enforced via CI/CD
