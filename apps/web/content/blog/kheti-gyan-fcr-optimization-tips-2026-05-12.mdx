## मुख्य बातें
- Industry benchmark FCR: 1.6–1.8 (Cobb 400, Ross 308)
- Poor FCR (>2.0) = ₹2-3/kg extra feed cost
- 5 key factors affecting FCR: feed quality, water, health, environment, management
- Daily monitoring essential for FCR improvement

## FCR क्या है और क्यों Important है

### FRC Definition
FCR = Total feed consumed (kg) / Total weight gain (kg)

**Example:**
- 25,000 birds consume 50,000 kg feed
- Total weight gain: 30,000 kg
- FCR = 50,000 / 30,000 = 1.67

**Good FCR:** 1.6–1.8 (Cobb 400, Ross 308 at 42 days)
**Poor FCR:** >2.0 (indicates problems)

### Financial Impact
At ₹2,000/ton feed cost:
- FCR 1.6: ₹3,200 feed cost per kg weight gain
- FCR 2.0: ₹4,000 feed cost per kg weight gain
- **Difference: ₹800/kg = ₹20,000 extra cost for 25,000 birds at 2kg avg weight**

## 5 Practical FCR Optimization Tips

### 1. Feed Quality Management
- **Buy from reputed suppliers**: Avoid adulterated feed
- **Check protein content**: 20-22% CP for starter, 18-20% for finisher
- **Store properly**: Cool, dry place, prevent mold
- **Use within 30 days**: Nutrient degradation over time

**Impact:** Proper feed quality can improve FCR by 0.1-0.2 points

### 2. Water Quality & Availability
- **Test water regularly**: pH 6.5-8.5, low bacterial count
- **Clean waterers daily**: Prevent biofilm buildup
- **Ensure 24/7 availability**: Water restriction = feed intake drop
- ** nipple drinker height**: Adjust weekly as birds grow

**Impact:** Clean water can improve FCR by 0.05-0.1 points

### 3. Health Management
- **Vaccination schedule**: Follow UP broiler protocol strictly
- **Biosecurity**: Limit visitor access, disinfect equipment
- **Monitor mortality daily**: Abnormal spikes indicate health issues
- **Coccidiosis prevention**: Regular anticoccidial in feed

**Impact:** Healthy birds have 0.1-0.15 better FCR than sick birds

### 4. Environment Control
- **Temperature**: 32-35°C (day 1), reduce 0.5°C daily to 22-24°C
- **Ventilation**: Remove ammonia, maintain oxygen levels
- **Lighting**: 23 hours light first week, then 20 hours
- **Stocking density**: Max 12-14 birds/sqm for good FCR

**Impact:** Optimal environment can improve FCR by 0.1-0.2 points

### 5. Daily Feed Management
- **Feed 4-5 times daily**: Prevent wastage, ensure fresh feed
- **Adjust based on age**: Follow breed-specific feeding program
- **Monitor feed intake**: Sudden drop = health/environment issue
- **Record daily**: Track feed per bird to identify trends

**Impact:** Proper feeding management can improve FCR by 0.05-0.1 points
