## Technical Details

### Model Performance Breakdown
- **Training data**: 5 years of historical data (2021-2026)
- **Features**: 45+ signals across 6 categories
- **Validation**: 30-day rolling window with manual ground-truthing
- **Deployment**: Daily inference at 6:00 AM IST, WhatsApp delivery by 6:30 AM

### Accuracy Calculation Methodology
1. **Daily prediction**: Model generates P50 price forecast at 6:00 AM
2. **Ground truth**: AGMARKNET published price at 6:00 PM same day
3. **Direction check**: Compare forecast vs actual price direction
4. **MAPE calculation**: |Predicted - Actual| / Actual × 100
5. **Conformal coverage**: % of actual prices within P10-P90 range

### Data Sources
- **AGMARKNET**: Daily mandi prices (all UP districts)
- **NECC**: National egg coordination committee rates
- **IMD**: Weather forecasts and historical data
- **NCDEX**: Maize and soya commodity prices
- **Festival calendar**: Religious and cultural events affecting demand
- **HPAI alerts**: Government disease outbreak notifications
