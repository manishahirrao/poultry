## HPAI क्या है और क्यों Matter करता है

### What is HPAI?
**HPAI (Highly Pathogenic Avian Influenza)** या "Bird Flu":
- Viral disease जो poultry birds को affect करती है
- High mortality rate (up to 90-100% in affected flocks)
- Rapid spread (airborne, contact, contaminated equipment)
- Government culling mandatory (affected zone में सभी birds को kill करना पड़ता है)

### Why It Matters to UP Farmers
**Financial impact:**
- **Price crash**: HPAI news से पहले ही price 20-30% drop हो जाता है
- **Culling loss**: अगर आपका flock affected है, 100% loss (government compensation limited)
- **Market closure**: Mandis close, transport stops
- **Consumer fear**: Demand drops for weeks even after outbreak controlled

**UP Context:**
- UP ने 2024 में multiple HPAI cases report किए (DADF data)
- Gorakhpur belt high-risk zone (high density, interstate transport)
- Nov 2024 में false rumor से ₹40/kg price drop हुआ था

## What to Do When You Receive an HPAI Alert

### If Severity: HIGH (Within 10km)
**Immediate Actions:**
1. **Contact local veterinary officer** — do not wait
2. **Isolate your flock** — no visitors, no new birds
3. **Monitor bird health** — check for symptoms (sudden death, respiratory distress)
4. **Prepare for culling** — if confirmed, government will order culling
5. **Document everything** — photos, records for compensation claim

**Financial Preparation:**
- Government compensation: ₹50-100/bird (varies by state)
- Insurance: Check if your policy covers disease outbreak
- Cash reserve: Keep emergency fund for such situations

### If Severity: MEDIUM (10-50km)
**Precautionary Actions:**
1. **Enhanced biosecurity** — disinfect vehicles, limit visitor access
2. **Monitor daily** — bird health, market news
3. **Do NOT panic-sell** — price may dip but will recover if you're not affected
4. **Stay updated** — check DADF reports daily

### If Severity: LOW (50km+)
**Normal Operations:**
1. **Business as usual** — no immediate action needed
2. **Stay informed** — monitor situation updates
3. **Market awareness** — price may dip slightly due to general fear
4. **Opportunity** — other farmers panic-selling — you can get better rates

## Government Compensation Scheme

### DADF HPAI Compensation Policy
**Eligibility:**
- Confirmed HPAI case in your flock (government-verified)
- Birds culled by government order
- Proper documentation (veterinary certificate, flock records)

**Compensation Rates (2024-25):**
- **Broilers**: ₹50-75/bird (varies by state)
- **Layers**: ₹100-150/bird
- **Processing time**: 30-60 days after culling

**Limitations:**
- Compensation is partial (does not cover full market value)
- Processing time means cash flow disruption
- Only for confirmed cases (not for price drops due to rumors)

**Why Early Warning Matters:**
- Compensation only covers confirmed cases
- Price drops due to rumors NOT covered
- Early warning helps you avoid panic-selling during rumors
