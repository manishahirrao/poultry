# PoultryPulse AI - Messaging Guidelines

## Brand Voice

- **Tone**: Helpful, trustworthy, farmer-first, practical
- **Language**: Hindi-primary with English subtitles where needed
- **Style**: Simple, direct, avoiding jargon
- **Emotion**: Empathetic to farmer challenges, optimistic about solutions

## Core Value Proposition

**Primary**: 95%+ सटीकता • 7-दिन भविष्यवाणी • गोरखपुर बेल्ट

**Secondary**: Built for farmers, by farmers

## Key Messaging Pillars

### 1. Accuracy
- 95%+ directional accuracy on price predictions
- 7-day advance notice on price movements
- Validated with real Gorakhpur farmers

### 2. Simplicity
- WhatsApp-first delivery (no new app needed)
- Hindi language support
- Easy-to-understand signals

### 3. Trust
- 24-hour response guarantee
- Refund if accuracy drops below 95%
- Transparent about methodology

## Page-Specific Messaging

### Homepage
- **Hero**: Focus on urgency and immediate benefit
- **CTA**: "14 दिन मुफ़्त — आज ही शुरू करें"
- **Social Proof**: Live counters, farmer testimonials

### Pricing Page
- **Value**: Emphasize ROI (₹3-5/kg profit per batch)
- **Trust**: "Best for" labels, customer testimonials per plan
- **Action**: Plan recommendation quiz for indecisive visitors

### About Page
- **Story**: Founder's personal connection to farming
- **Impact**: Real metrics (200+ farmers, 95%+ accuracy)
- **Team**: Highlight local Gorakhpur expertise

### How It Works
- **Process**: Simple 3-step explanation
- **Technical**: Simplified language (no jargon)
- **Demo**: "See It In Action" section

### FAQ Page
- **Popular**: Highlight most common questions
- **Objections**: Customer quotes addressing concerns
- **Action**: Both contact and signup CTAs

### Contact Page
- **Segmentation**: Sales vs Support vs Partnership
- **Response**: 24-hour guarantee
- **Personal**: Team photos, office location

## Terminology Guidelines

### Use
- "भाव" (price/market rate)
- "बैच" (batch of birds)
- "संकेत" (signal/alert)
- "फायदा" (profit/benefit)

### Avoid
- Complex ML/AI terminology
- Financial jargon
- English-only messaging
- Overly technical explanations

## CTA Guidelines

### Primary CTAs
- "14 दिन मुफ़्त शुरू करें"
- "Demo बुक करें"
- "WhatsApp पर संपर्क करें"

### Secondary CTAs
- "और जानें"
- "देखें कैसे काम करता है"
- "सवाल पूछें"

## Social Proof Messaging

### Testimonials
- Include farmer name, location, flock size
- Highlight specific outcomes (₹ saved, % improvement)
- Use real Hindi quotes where possible

### Metrics
- 200+ farmers in Gorakhpur belt
- 95%+ prediction accuracy
- ₹3-5/kg average profit per batch
- 47 data sources integrated

## Geographic Focus

- Primary: Gorakhpur belt (Gorakhpur, Deoria, Kushinagar)
- Secondary: Uttar Pradesh (10 districts)
- Future: Pan-India poultry-producing states

3. Ensure consistency across all pages
4. Test with target audience (farmers)
